## Daily Rollup

ANET makes it easy to see recent reports. At anytime, you can click on “Daily Rollup” on the
left-hand menu and see the reports that have been approved within the selected period. You can
scroll through the report summaries, and click on the “Read full report” link to see the entire
report.
